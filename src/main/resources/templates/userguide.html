Login & Authentication

Secure dual-authentication for Teachers and Students

MTNG uses Spring Security with BCrypt password hashing. Both Teachers and Students log in through the same page — the system auto-detects your role.

Meeting Management

Start, monitor, and stop meetings with one click

1
Start a Meeting
Click ▶ Start Meeting in the header. A unique Jitsi room is auto-generated.
2
Monitor Live Stats
Watch real-time counters: In Meeting, Online, and Total students.
3
Enable Full Recording
Open ⚙ Settings → ⏺ Full Meeting Recording to auto-save recordings for all participants when the meeting ends.
4
End the Meeting
Click ⏹ Stop Meeting. Recordings auto-save, the session is logged, participant tracking is cleared.
⚠️
Important: Only one meeting can be active at a time. Starting a new one automatically stops the previous meeting.

FAQ & Troubleshooting

Common questions and solutions

🔸 Why is my data gone after restart?

H2 is an in-memory database with create-drop mode — data resets on restart. For persistence, switch to PostgreSQL/MySQL.

🔸 How do I reset all data?

Restart the app. DataInitializer re-seeds sample data automatically.

🔸 Can students on other devices access the app?

Yes! Same network required. Use the WhatsApp feature to share the auto-detected LAN URL.

🔸 "Access Denied" on Create Student?

You need Admin role. Log in with admin / admin123.

🔸 Meeting room shows "No students in meeting"?

Students must navigate to /meeting-room and join. Ensure admin has started a meeting.

🔸 How do I run tests?
./mvnw test
🔸 How do I build a production JAR?
./mvnw clean package -DskipTests java -jar target/Mtng-0.0.1-SNAPSHOT.jar
🔴
Known Limitation: CSRF is disabled for /api/**. In production, enable CSRF for APIs or use JWT authentication.
